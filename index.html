Popular Destinations

NEW DELHI

Delhi, the capital of India, is one of the oldest cities in the world, steeped in rich history and cultural diversity. It is home to iconic monuments like the Red Fort, Lotus Temple, and the majestic Qutub Minar1

Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the capital city of the Indian state of Telangana, known for its unique blend of Hindi, Telugu, and Urdu cultures. Often referred to as the “City of Pearls”, Hyderabad has a rich history, iconic monuments like the Charminar, and once thrived as a hub for pearl and diamond trading 1

Mumbai

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is the financial capital of India and a bustling metropolis on the western coast. It boasts iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India, vibrant neighborhoods, and a thriving film industry known as Bollywood

Featured Hotels

THE OBEROI RAJVILAS

The Oberoi Rajvilas Jaipur, India 2,640 reviews Jaipur, 'The Pink City', was the vision and triumph of Maharaja Jai Singh II, warrior, astronomer and politician. Built in 1727, it has at its centre the City Palace from which radiate the oleander pink buildings, domes and minarets

THE LEELA PALACE

The Leela Palace New Delhi New Delhi, India 5,794 reviews The Leela Palace New Delhi at Chanakyapuri is located in the exclusive Diplomatic Enclave and has room sizes starting from 520 Sq ft. The hotel is close to the seat of government and houses four specialty restaurants and three bars, a multilevel spa with a fully equipped fitness center and the city's only rooftop temperature-controlled swimming pool

The Savoy Mussoorie

Mussoorie, India With a regal history of more than a hundred years, the hotel has been delighting famous dignitaries and royalties from India and abroad with its heart-warming services and all the appurtenances of luxury. As a summer resort in the heydays of the Raj, The Savoy at Mussoorie had few equals. More than a hundred years later, it still is. Today, as a WelcomHotel, it stands in all its glory, ready to turn the clock back in time for the connoisseur of fine living
